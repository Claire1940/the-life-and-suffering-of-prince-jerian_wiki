The Life and Suffering of Prince Jerian is a narrative RPG that takes players through the trials and tribulations of Prince Jerian as he navigates the complex political landscape of the Blessed Arknian Empire. The game's unique blend of political strategy and character development creates a deeply immersive experience where players must make tough decisions that will shape the fate of the empire.
