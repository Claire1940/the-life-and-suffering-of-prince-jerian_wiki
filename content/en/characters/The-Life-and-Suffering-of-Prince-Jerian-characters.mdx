## Characters Guide

### Prince Jerian
- **Role**: The main protagonist, Prince Jerian is the heir to the throne of the Blessed Arknian Empire.
- **Personality**: Ambitious, intelligent, and determined, but also compassionate and principled.
- **Relationships**: Jerian has relationships with various factions, including the nobility, the church, and the common people.

### Noble Faction Leader
- **Role**: A representative of the empire's aristocratic families, valuing tradition and influence.
- **Personality**: Dignified, ambitious, and often conservative.

- **Role**: A high-ranking religious authority, representing the Church's influence over imperial politics.

- **Relationships**: The church official can provide stability, but may require difficult compromises.

### Common People Representative
- **Role**: A voice for ordinary citizens affected by taxation, laws, and royal decisions.

- **Relationships**: The common people representative can improve public trust and create pressure for reform.

### Royal Family Member
- **Role**: A member of the imperial household connected to Jerian's personal life and succession concerns.

### Political Rival

## FAQ

**What happens if I regret my class choice?**
Once you select a class in "The Life and Suffering of Prince Jerian," the decision is permanent. However, you can create a new character if you want to explore a different class.

**Can I play as a Mage but use a sword?**
Yes, but it won't be as effective as choosing a class that complements that playstyle, like Knight or Rogue.

**Which class is best for solo play?**
Mage is ideal for solo play due to its powerful spells and ranged attacks. However, Rogue also works well if you want more mobility and hybrid play.
