### 1. Introduction to The Life and Suffering of Prince Jerian

The Life and Suffering of Prince Jerian is a narrative RPG that takes players through the political and personal struggles of Prince Jerian, the heir to the throne of the Blessed Arknian Empire. The game is known for its deep political narrative and multiple endings, making it a must-play for fans of the genre.

#### Game Basics

- **Genre**: Narrative RPG
- **Platform**: PC Steam
- **Developer**: Schisma Games
- **Publisher**: 101XP
- **Release Date**: July 20, 2026

#### Story Overview

The game follows Prince Jerian as he navigates the complex political landscape of the Arknian Empire. With a focus on political decisions and character development, players will make choices that shape the fate of the empire and Jerian's own destiny.

### 2. Understanding the Political System

One of the key aspects of The Life and Suffering of Prince Jerian is its political system. Players will need to understand the various factions and their interests in order to make informed decisions.

#### Factions

- **Nobility**: Values tradition and influence.
- **Church**: Has significant influence over imperial politics.
- **Common People**: Represents the interests of ordinary citizens.
- **Royal Family**: Members connected to Jerian's personal life and succession.

#### Making Political Decisions

Players will be faced with difficult decisions that can have long-term consequences. It's important to consider the impact of each decision on the various factions and Jerian's own reputation.

### 3. The Impact of Choices

The game's narrative is heavily influenced by the choices players make. Different decisions can lead to different endings, making it a game worth replaying to experience all possible outcomes.

#### Endings

- **Happy Ending**: Jerian's decisions lead to a peaceful and prosperous empire.
- **Sad Ending**: Jerian's decisions lead to war, chaos, or his own downfall.
- **Multiple Endings**: Players can explore different paths and outcomes by making different choices.

### 4. Character Development

Jerian's character grows and changes throughout the game. Players will need to manage his attributes, such as strength, agility, intellect, willpower, charisma, and sanity, to ensure he is prepared for the challenges ahead.

#### Attributes

- **Strength**: Represents physical capability and authority.
- **Agility**: Influences adaptability and quick responses.
- **Intellect**: Helps unlock analytical choices and strategic decisions.
- **Willpower**: Represents resistance against pressure and difficult sacrifices.
- **Charisma**: Improves diplomacy and influence over important characters.
- **Sanity**: Reflects Jerian's mental condition and affects decision-making in stressful situations.
