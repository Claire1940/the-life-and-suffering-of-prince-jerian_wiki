The game features a rich narrative with multiple endings, allowing players to experience the story from different perspectives. Players make choices that affect the outcome of the game, including political decisions, personal relationships, and moral dilemmas.

Players must navigate the political landscape of the Blessed Arknian Empire, making decisions that can influence the fate of the empire and its people.
