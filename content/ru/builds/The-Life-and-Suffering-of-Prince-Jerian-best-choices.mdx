In the first chapter, you are introduced to Prince Jerian and the empire he will one day rule. This is where you start making choices that will have long-term consequences. 

**Callout:**

### Stats to Consider:
- **Logic vs. Passion**: Choose based on how you want to approach the challenges ahead. Logic can help you make calculated decisions, while passion can drive you to act on impulse.
- **Will vs. Blood**: Willpower helps you push through tough times, while blood can make you more prone to rash actions.

## FAQ

**Question: What happens if I regret my class choice?**
Answer: Once you select a class in "The Life and Suffering of Prince Jerian," the decision is permanent. However, you can create a new character if you want to explore a different class.

**Question: Can I play as a Mage but use a sword?**
Answer: Yes, but it won't be as effective as choosing a class that complements that playstyle, like Knight or Rogue.

**Question: Which class is best for solo play?**
Answer: Mage is ideal for solo play due to its powerful spells and ranged attacks. However, Rogue also works well if you want more mobility and hybrid play.
